Glass Guard: Universal Coating For All Devices

  • GlassGuard

    Nano Liquid Glass Protection

    Glass Guard is a universal protective ultra coating for all watch, phone or tablet surfaces. Glass Guard pre-treated wipes are suitable for all sizes of electronic device and leave an almost diamond hard finish, giving entire surface protection.

  • Application

    It couldn't be easier

    Each Glass Guard pack consists of two easy to use pre-treated sachets. Firstly apply the Nano Clean pre-treated wipe to ensure a pristine screen prior to applying Glass Guard. The Glass Guard pre-treated wipe should then be applied evenly but not excessively.

  • 100% Bubble Free

    Entire surface protection

    Nano technology reacts immediately, bonding to the glass surface. When the Glass Guard wipe is dry your device is ready for use and your glass surface has an incredible five year coating warranty.

  • Almost Diamond Hard Finish

    Five year coating warranty

    Enjoy the HD experience of your screen's display. More touch screen sensitive. Reduce finger marks and smudges. Your screen is easier to clean. All thanks to Glass Guard. Order your pack today.
