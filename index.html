About

Glass Guard: Universal Coating For All Devices

  • GlassGuard

    Nano Liquid Glass Protection

    It's estimated that about 20% of people walk around with a broken screen on their smart phone, not surprising when 90% of us admit to dropping our phones at least once a month. Sick of costly repairs we thought that there had to be a better way of protecting our devices. The stick on protectors were fiddly to apply, attracted dirt and rarely had no bubbles on the screen. After a lot of investigation and even more testing we discovered Nano Technology and came up with Glass Guard.

  • Application

    It couldn't be easier

    Glass Guard is an easy to apply Nano Liquid. It's one wipe application bonds with your devices screen to give you a layer of protection that is almost as hard as a diamond.

  • How Glass Guard Works

    The Glass Guard matrix bonds with your screen to a liquid glass layer that will offer give your device the screen the ultimate protection. Glass Guard offers unbeatable unbeatable protection for up to 5 years. Glass Guard has been developed not only to be ultra durable but also flexible and breathable. This means that there is flex within the glass for ultimate impact protection.

  • Benefits of Glass Guard

    • Protection from scratches for up to 5 years
    • High gloss finish
    • Increased sensitivity of your touch screen
    • Reduced smudges and fingerprints all of which will give your HD display more clarity
    • Water repellent, germ and dirt repellent
    • Highly resistant to a large number of organic solvents
    • Environmentally and Food safe
    • Heat resistant up to 750°C working temperatures
    • Cold resistant up to -90°C

Smart Phone

GlassGuard Nano Liquid Protector

THE Ultimate Nano Liquid Screen Protection Technology This easy to apply screen protector offers your device unbeatable, long lasting protection from scratches. There are no longer issues of bubbles or peeling screens like previous generation screen protectors. Glass Guard is applied with a pre-treated wipe, once set this nano liquid protection will be almost as hard as a diamond. The Glass Guard matrix bonds with your screen to a liquid glass layer that will offer give your device screen the ultimate protection.

Glass Guard bonds with your screen to offer unbeatable protection for up to 5 years, a high gloss finish, increased sensitivity of your touch screen, reduced smudges and fingerprints all of which will give your HD display more clarity. All in addition to being water repellent, germ and dirt repellent.

Glass Guard is available in three handy sizes to suit the surface you are treating Smartwatch, Smart Phone and Tablet size. This will ensure that you have the right amount of Glass Guard in our pre-treated wipe for your device. In addition our pack includes a NANOKLEEN wipe to ensure that your screen in thoroughly clean before applying.

Ultimate Nano Coating Technology - Si02 is considered the bench mark for NANO technology in coatings. Advanced Nano Coating developed a unique compound incorporating SiO4 and SiH4 to create a true Hybrid Nano coating unequalled Durability, Strength, Clarity, Hygiene and Permanent Protection. High Impact Strength - GlassGuard has been developed not only to be ultra durable but also flexible and breathable. This strengthens yet maintains flex within the glass for ultimate impact protection. Certified 9H Scratch Resistance - On the Mohs scale of mineral hardness which determines scratch resistance, Talcum powder is 1H and a Diamond is 10H. GlassGuard Certified 9H is impressively hard.

Permanent Protection - When we talk permanent we mean it. A coated surface will remain coated for up to 5 years
