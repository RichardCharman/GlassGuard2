Glass-Guard at The Gadget Show Live

The Gadget Show Live is the home of innovation in tech world. With all the major brands making an appearance it was natural for Glass-Guard to attend and bring Nano Technology to the consumer market. It was great to meet so many people who were passionate about their devices and protecting them. As Glass-Guard is such a new way to protect, this was a great opportunity for us to explain exactly what nano technology was and field any questions.

Our nano coating experts were kept extremely busy throughout the show. They loved meeting new customers and coating some beautiful devices. Our video library has some new clips of Glass-Guard being applied to a number of devices, check it out. We saw a huge number of Samsung S7 Edges at the show and were able to coat both front and back of them. Glass-Guard is ideal for use with the S7, it's such a beautiful device any film or tempered glass would really take away from the look.

Thanks to everyone who came to see at the Gadget Show Live. If you missed us at make sure to follow us on Twitter/Facebook for updates on other shows we will be at this year.
